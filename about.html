About Zamani Impact Lab

A bold experiment in local innovation

Who We Are

Zamani Impact Lab is a bold experiment in local innovation.

We are a collaborative platform where ideas meet data, where youth creativity powers solutions, and where development challenges are reimagined through the lens of people who live them.

Our mission is rooted in one belief: the answers we seek for sustainable development already exist in our communities—they only need the space, tools, and trust to grow.

From the heart of Northern Nigeria, we create room for new thinking. We support unconventional ideas, challenge outdated systems, and scale what works.

Local Innovation

We believe in the power of local solutions. Our approach starts with understanding community needs and building from within, not imposing external solutions.

Collaborative Platform

We bring together diverse voices—techies, teachers, tailors, and traditional leaders—to create unexpected partnerships that drive real change.

Evidence-Based Impact

We back data with stories, and stories with numbers, ensuring our innovations are both meaningful and measurable.

What Makes Us Different

We don't just run programs—we design impact. Our approach is experimental, evidence-based, and community-centered. We create space for new thinking and support ideas that challenge the status quo.

  • We start with questions, not assumptions
  • We design with communities, not for them
  • We focus on simple, scalable, and sustainable solutions
  • We connect unlikely allies for maximum impact
