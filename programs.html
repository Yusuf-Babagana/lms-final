Our Programs

Innovative initiatives designed to create lasting impact

Designing Impact Through Innovation

Our programs are carefully designed to foster innovation, build capacity, and create sustainable solutions that address real community needs. Each initiative reflects our commitment to community-driven development and evidence-based impact.

The Impact Forge

A rapid design & prototyping lab

The Impact Forge is where ideas transform into actionable solutions. This intensive program brings together innovators, community members, and technical experts to rapidly design, prototype, and test solutions to pressing development challenges.

Key Features: Rapid prototyping, community co-design, technical mentorship, real-world testing

Zamani Data Circle

A youth-powered data initiative

Empowering young people to become data champions in their communities. The Data Circle trains youth in data collection, analysis, and storytelling to ensure community voices are heard in decision-making processes.

Key Features: Data literacy training, community research projects, evidence-based advocacy, youth leadership development

Frontline Futures Fellowship

Supporting bold thinkers

An intensive fellowship program for individuals with innovative ideas for addressing development challenges. Fellows receive funding, mentorship, and access to networks to turn their bold visions into reality.

Key Features: Funding support, expert mentorship, peer learning networks, implementation guidance

Idea-to-Action Labs

High-impact sprints to pilot solutions

Intensive, focused sessions where teams work on specific challenges using human-centered design principles. These labs move quickly from problem identification to solution piloting in real community settings.

Key Features: Human-centered design, rapid piloting, community partnership, iterative improvement

The SDG Sandbox

A creative platform for testing new frameworks

An experimental space for testing innovative approaches to achieving the Sustainable Development Goals. The Sandbox encourages unconventional thinking and provides a safe space for trying new methodologies.

Key Features: Experimental methodologies, cross-sector collaboration, innovative frameworks, impact measurement

Program Impact

Our programs are designed to create multiple layers of impact - from individual capacity building to systemic change. We measure success through community ownership, scalability, and sustainable outcomes.

Individual Level

Building skills, confidence, and networks among participants to become effective change agents in their communities.

Community Level

Strengthening local capacity for innovation and problem-solving while addressing specific community challenges.

Systemic Level

Creating new models and approaches that can be scaled and replicated across different contexts and regions.
