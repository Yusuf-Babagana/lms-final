Mission & Vision

Guiding principles that drive our impact

Our Mission

To ignite community-driven innovation that advances the SDGs through collaboration, experimentation, and evidence.

Community-Driven Innovation

We believe innovation starts with communities. Our mission centers on empowering local voices to lead change and create solutions from within.

Advancing the SDGs

Every initiative we support contributes to the Sustainable Development Goals, creating measurable impact toward global targets through local action.

Evidence & Experimentation

We combine rigorous experimentation with data-driven evidence to ensure our innovations are both effective and scalable.

Our Vision

A connected ecosystem where local voices lead, ideas thrive, and inclusive solutions reshape development.

Connected Ecosystem

We envision a network of innovators, communities, and organizations working together across boundaries to create lasting change.

Local Voices Lead

A future where communities are not just beneficiaries but drivers of development, with the agency and resources to shape their own destiny.

Ideas Thrive

An environment where unconventional thinking is celebrated, bold ideas receive support, and innovation is accessible to all.

Our Core Values

  • Collaboration: We believe in the power of partnerships and co-creation
  • Innovation: We embrace unconventional thinking and experimental approaches
  • Community-Centered: Local voices and needs drive everything we do
  • Evidence-Based: We ground our work in data and measurable outcomes
  • Inclusivity: We ensure diverse perspectives shape our solutions
  • Sustainability: We focus on long-term impact and scalable solutions

    • How We Measure Success

    Our success is measured not just by the programs we run, but by the lasting change we enable in communities. We track our impact through:

    Community Ownership

    The degree to which communities take ownership of initiatives and continue driving them forward independently.

    Scalable Solutions

    The number of locally-developed solutions that successfully scale to impact broader populations.

    Systemic Change

    Evidence of shifts in systems, policies, and approaches that enable lasting, structural improvements.
